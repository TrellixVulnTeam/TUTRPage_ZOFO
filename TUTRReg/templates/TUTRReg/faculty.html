So what do all these people do? In a nutshell...

Governor
  • University oversight. Guides and fills all other TUTR roles as needed. Accountable to the Principality.
  • Operations
  • Training
  • Reporting
Deputy Governor - Support for the Governor, training to take over the role as Governor.

Registrar
  • Responsible for Student Records, data entry into the system.
  • Database support (delegate office) - build/maintain the database
Deans or Degree Administrators
  • Each Degree or "school" will have a Dean. These Deans don't have to be experts on the Degree know who to reach out to for assistance.
  • Helps Session Organizers (Branch Officers and/or TUTR Administrators*) find teachers for a particular subject
  • Reviews teacher evaluations
  • Provides unbiased feedback to teachers (removes who said what before giving feedback)
  • Point of contact for the degree program for all of Tir Righ
  • Point of contact for teachers to ensure that classes taught meet, in general, the credit requirements for a degree.
  • Provides assistance to the Registrar regarding classes tracked in the database
  • Reviews records, makes recommendations to the Governor for degree candidates

Branch Officers

  • Branch Officers are optional to the local branch and certainly not required. They are a part of the local branch Council.
  • Local councils can recommend a Branch Officer but the Governor warrants them. Just like the College of Heralds.
  • Can also be session facilitators or supervise TUTR facilitators.
  • Works with local event stewards to ensure classes are held at events.
  • Can also be session facilitators or supervise TUTR facilitators.
  • Provides reports to the both the local Seneschal(e) and the Governor on local activities quarterly.
