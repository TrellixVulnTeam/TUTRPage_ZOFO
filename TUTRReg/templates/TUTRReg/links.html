University of Ithra, Kingdom of An Tir

The University of Ithra is the Kingdom of An Tir's university. Ithra degrees are Lector Artis (“Reader in Arts”) and Lector Scientae (“Reader in Sciences”). 

Some credits from University of Ithra courses are transferrable to your degree with The University of Tir Righ. See TUTR Freqently Asked Questions for more details on transfer credits.

THUA (Their Highness' University of Avacal)

Their Highness' University of Avacal and The University of Tir Righ offer a joint session at the Avacal-Tir Righ war each summer.

Some credits from Their Highness' University of Avacal courses are transferrable to your degree with The University of Tir Righ. See TUTR Freqently Asked Questions for more details on transfer credits.
